Vegetable Soup

This vegetable soup recipe is a low-fat but filling tomato-based vegetable soup.
I use fresh vegetables when in season, frozen or canned in winter.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes:1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • Cocktail:1 (11.5 ounce) can tomato-vegetable juice cocktail
  • Carrots:2 carrots, sliced
  • Celery:2 stalks celery, diced
  • Potato:1 large potato, diced
  • Green Beans:1 cup chopped fresh green beans
  • Corn:1 cup fresh corn kernels
  • Water:1 cup water

    • Steps

    1. Combine celery, onion, carrots, tomatoes, tomato-vegetable juice, water, leek,
      potato, peas, corn, garbanzo beans, rice, soy sauce, thyme, ground black pepper,
      garlic powder, and dill weed in a large pot over high heat.
    2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until vegetables are tender, about 30 minutes
