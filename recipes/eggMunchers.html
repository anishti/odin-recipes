Egg Munchers

A light spinach, egg, and cottage cheese casserole that's great for brunch.

Ingredients

  • Eggs:6 eggs, beaten
  • Onion:¾ cup onion, chopped
  • Flour:¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • Spinach:1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • Cheese:1 (16 ounce) package small curd cottage cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese:1 (16 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese
  • butter:½ cup butter, melted
  • Salt and Pepper:salt and pepper to taste

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking
      dish with cooking spray.
    2. Beat eggs, onion, and flour in a mixing bowl until flour is no longer lumpy.
      Stir in spinach, cottage cheese, Cheddar cheese, butter, salt, and pepper
      until evenly combined; pour into the prepared dish.
